Battery-Free Eye Tracker on Glasses

Summary: What if by tracking our eye movement, we can optimize graphic rendering in augmented reality or monitor our health? DartNets lab presents a wearable eye tracker on glasses to do all that.

Role: videomaker; individual project

Timeline: 2 weeks (September 2018)

Context: Dartmouth College, Computer Science Department

Description: The eye-tracker works by sensing how your eye reflects light using sensors around the eye. Because the pupil absorbs light, its movement and size variations change the reflected light that the sensors detect.

Challenge: Present the complicated information about the engineering behind the eye tracking device and in a simple manner for the general public. All in one minute.
